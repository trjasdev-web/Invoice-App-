# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: January 31, 2026**

This Privacy Policy describes how Invoice App ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our mobile application ("App"). By using our App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Information You Provide

#### Business Information
- Business name
- GSTIN (Goods and Services Tax Identification Number)
- Business address
- State code
- Bank account details (for display on invoices)

#### Client/Customer Information
- Client name
- Client GSTIN
- Client address and state code
- Email address
- Phone number

#### Product/Service Information
- Product or service names
- HSN/SAC codes
- Unit of measurement
- Pricing information
- GST rates

#### Invoice Data
- Invoice numbers
- Invoice dates and due dates
- Line items and amounts
- Tax calculations (CGST, SGST, IGST)
- Payment status
- Notes

#### Visual Content
- Business logo (uploaded from gallery/camera or generated)
- Digital signature (drawn or uploaded)

### 1.2 Information Collected Automatically

#### Device Information
- Device type and model
- Operating system version
- Unique device identifiers

#### Usage Information
- App features used
- Interaction patterns
- Error logs and crash reports

### 1.3 Information from Third-Party Services

#### Google Account Information
When you sign in with Google, we receive:
- Google account ID
- Email address
- Display name
- Profile photo URL

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

### 2.1 Provide Core Services
- Create and manage GST-compliant invoices
- Store and organize client information
- Maintain product/service catalogs
- Generate PDF invoices for sharing and printing
- Calculate GST (CGST, SGST, IGST) automatically

### 2.2 Data Backup and Sync
- Sync your data across devices using Google Sheets (with your permission)
- Store backup copies in your Google Drive
- Enable offline-first functionality with automatic sync when online

### 2.3 Improve Our Services
- Analyze app usage to improve features
- Fix bugs and technical issues
- Develop new features based on user needs

---

## 3. Data Storage and Security

### 3.1 Local Storage
- All your data is stored locally on your device using encrypted Hive database
- The App works offline-first, ensuring data availability without internet

### 3.2 Cloud Storage (Optional)
- When you enable Google Sync, data is stored in your personal Google Drive
- Data is synced to Google Sheets in your own Google account
- We do not have access to your Google Drive or Sheets data

### 3.3 Security Measures
- Data encryption at rest on your device
- Secure OAuth 2.0 authentication with Google
- No transmission of sensitive data to our servers
- All cloud data remains in your personal Google account

## 6. Your Rights and Choices

- Export invoices as PDF documents

### 6.2 Data Deletion
